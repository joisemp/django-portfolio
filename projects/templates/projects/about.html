About

My name is Joise, I am a designer and web developer based in Kerala, India

I started doing daily UI practices in March 2021, and since then I learned UI design along with Web Devolopment.

I'm planning to get better at development and pick up blogging as well, so lots of exciting things to look out for!

If you're interested in my journey stay tuned, and if you'd like to get in touch feel free to drop me a message.
